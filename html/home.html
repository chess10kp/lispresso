    ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- About Us and Lisp-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    We are a company dedicated to reviving Lisp, a pioneering programming language introduced by John McCarthy in the late 1950s. Our goal is to honor its legacy by offering people opportunities to learn and explore this influential language by utilizing our interactive practice course.

    Lisp's legacy is significant, having introduced foundational programming concepts like symbolic expressions, recursion, dynamic typing, and garbage collection. Its code-as-data philosophy and flexible macro system inspired the development of functional programming and influenced many modern languages. Notably important in artificial intelligence research, Lisp's innovative ideas continue to impact fields requiring symbolic computation.
